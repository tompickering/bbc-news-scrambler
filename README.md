# BBC News Scrambler

Some years ago, I experienced a persistent bug in the BBC News app for Android which sometimes caused the headline of one article to be displayed with the image of a completely different article.

The results were often hilarious, and I was dismayed when it was fixed.

Ever since, I've had the notion of writing a small utility to emulate the bug and revive the halcyon days of entertainingly mismatched headline-image pairings.

Here it is!


## Requirements

[bbc-news](https://pypi.org/project/bbc-news/)


## Disclaimer

The author accepts no responsibility for the unfortunate event that any particular randomly-generated headline-image pairing could be readily perceived as hideously offensive, shockingly distasteful - or worse, entirely unamusing.
